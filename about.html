Our Mission & The Mind Behind It

Understanding who we are is understanding why we are so committed to your success.

Our Guiding Purpose

"To serve as a catalyst for sustainable human and organizational flourishing. We achieve this by building a synergistic ecosystem where rigorous social science research, evidence-based well-being practices, and purpose-driven consulting converge to empower individuals and organizations to reach their full potential and create a lasting, positive legacy."

This mission is not just a statement; it is the operational blueprint for everything we do. We believe that true success is sustainable, that well-being is a driver of performance, and that every organization has the potential to make a profound, positive impact on the world.

Meet Our Founder

Şener Cem Irmak, Founder & CEO

Şener Cem Irmak is a psychologist, systemic counsellor, and the visionary founder of Koru Impact. He is driven by a dedication to promoting mental health and fostering organizational flourishing through a unique synthesis of evidence-based consulting and systemic empathy.

His rare, multi-disciplinary perspective is a direct result of a unique academic journey. Şener holds a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering alongside a Bachelor's in Psychology from Bilkent University, a Master's in Neurobiology and Behavior from Universität Bielefeld, and a Master's in Marriage and Family Therapy/Counseling from Istanbul Medeniyet University. This powerful combination of technology, psychology, and systems thinking allows him to address the challenges of the modern workplace with unparalleled depth and insight.

His extensive professional experience includes roles as a Well-being Specialist for global firms like Zevo Health, a counselor for the British International Schools of Istanbul, and a Provisional Psychologist for the Turkish Armed Forces. This diverse journey has given him deep insights into individual, group, and workplace well-being interventions across a wide range of populations. As the founder of Koru Impact, Şener now integrates his full spectrum of expertise to help organizations build healthier, more resilient, and more impactful cultures.
